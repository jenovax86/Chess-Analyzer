# Chess Analyzer
### A Simple Chess analyzer that analyzes your chess.com profile and gives you insights about your performance.


## Features
 - Show your performance in all time formats.
 - Show your rating over time.
 - Analyzes your accuracy in all time formats.
 - Estimates your win and loss.
